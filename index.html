Particle Collisions
Use slider to control volume of the game

Elastic means No Sound

Particles within the container will collide without losing their kinetic energy. Their kinetic energy may transfer to one another, but the net KE will conserve. However, as soon as you turn up the volume. Kinetic energy will no longer conserve. The energy will dissipate through sound. Eventually, these particles will come to a stop.

The Math

Please keep in mind, all variables are vectors except that mass is scalar. The x's denote the position of the two particle at the instance of impact.
